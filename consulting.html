I help early-stage engineering teams get their infrastructure to a place where AI can actually make a difference: clean release processes, solid monitoring, and the right amount of automation. No more, no less.

  • You don't have a dedicated infrastructure lead — it's owned by whoever has time, or nobody

Infrastructure Cleanup

From $25,000
4–8 weeks  ·  Fixed fee  ·  Scoped per engagement

Once you know what needs fixing, I can do the work. A Cleanup engagement tackles one well-defined problem: getting your infrastructure into Terraform, migrating your observability stack, untangling your release process, or right-sizing your cloud spend.

  • Legacy data warehouse migration to Snowflake with microservices ETL
